# Art asset attribution & disclaimer

The image assets in `wiki/` (playing-card faces, Joker art, boss-blind icons, planet/
consumable cards, enhancement/edition/seal art) are artwork from the video game **Balatro**.

- **Balatro** is © 2024 **LocalThunk**, published by **Playstack**.
- These images were sourced from the community-run **Balatro Wiki** (<https://balatrowiki.org>)
  via its MediaWiki API; see `../fetch_wiki_assets.py` for exactly what was fetched.

They are included here **only** as a visualization aid for this non-commercial, educational
reinforcement-learning research project's replay viewer — to make agent replays easier to
read and debug. This use is believed to fall under fair use / fan-project norms.

**This project is a fan-made research tool. It is not affiliated with, authorized by, or
endorsed by LocalThunk or Playstack.** All rights to the Balatro artwork remain with their
respective owners. If you are a rights holder and would like these assets removed, please
open an issue on the repository and they will be taken down.

To refresh or re-fetch the assets: `python -m balatro_rl.viz.fetch_wiki_assets`.
